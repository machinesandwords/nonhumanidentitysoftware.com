About This Site

No Switzerland

The NHI security market is new enough that the usual independent infrastructure hasn't caught up. The content that exists is mostly vendor-produced. The analyst coverage is mostly opaque. The media coverage is mostly sponsored. This site is the thing that should exist but doesn't: practitioner-oriented, vendor-neutral coverage of a market that's moving fast and matters.

What independent actually means here

No vendor relationships. No sponsored content. No affiliate arrangements. No advertising. Vendors are included because they're significant to buyers in the market, not because they paid for inclusion or requested it.

That means the vendor index includes platforms with weak products. The comparisons say who loses. The guides address the operational problems that vendor documentation skips because acknowledging them would complicate the sales process.

This site takes positions. Neutral analysis that refuses to say which platform wins and which doesn't isn't useful to someone making a procurement decision. When this site has an opinion, it says so. When the evidence is thin or genuinely contested, it says that too.

Who publishes this

Non-Human Identity Software is published by Machines & Words, an independent research and media operation covering B2B software markets. The NHI Register newsletter covers the market biweekly for practitioners and buyers. Subscribe here.

How coverage decisions get made

Vendors appear in the index because they're significant to enterprise buyers. Significance is assessed on funding and traction, adoption in enterprise environments, and whether the platform does something meaningfully distinct from the rest of the market. Platforms that exist primarily as marketing operations without real enterprise customers are not included regardless of funding level.

Comparisons are written for scenarios where both platforms are genuinely co-evaluated by real buyers. They don't exist because a vendor requested one or because co-evaluation is theoretically possible. When a comparison doesn't belong, because the platforms serve genuinely different buyers or solve genuinely different problems, this site says so instead of writing a forced competition.

Corrections

The NHI market moves fast. Funding rounds, acquisitions, and product changes happen frequently. When something here is wrong or out of date, corrections are made. If you have specific factual corrections, submit them here.
