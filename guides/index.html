NHI Security Guides: Past the Basics

Vendor documentation explains how a product works. It doesn't explain why NHI discovery stalls when you can't find anyone who owns the credentials you've found, or why your secrets management program is technically functional but still leaves a large portion of your NHI attack surface ungoverned. These guides address the operational problems vendor content skips.

These guides assume you're past the "what is NHI" stage. They're for practitioners dealing with the implementation problems that come after the initial deployment.
