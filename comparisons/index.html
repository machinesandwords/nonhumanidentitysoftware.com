NHI Security Platform Comparisons

Vendor positioning in this market is genuinely confusing. Every NHI governance platform claims broad coverage. Every secrets manager claims to address "the full NHI lifecycle." These comparisons cut through that. Each one covers a pairing where the two platforms are genuinely co-evaluated by real buyers, and each one tells you directly which one wins in which environment.

Start with the vendor index to understand the full market. Come back here once you have a shortlist and need to understand the specific trade-offs between two platforms. Each comparison is written for the buyer decision, not the marketing narrative.
